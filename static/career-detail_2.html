We're looking for a creative and technically skilled AI Prompt Engineer to join our AI & Innovation team. You'll design, test, and optimize prompts for generative AI tools including image, video, and 3D asset generation to accelerate our creative pipelines and deliver stunning AI-assisted content for clients worldwide.

Responsibilities
  • Design and refine prompts for AI image generators (Midjourney,Stable Diffusion, DALL-E) to produce production-quality visual assets
  • Develop prompt workflows for AI video generation tools (Runway, Pika, Sora) aligned with project creative briefs
  • Collaborate closely with 3D artists, designers, and animators to integrate AI outputs into existing pipelines
  • Research and evaluate emerging AI models, tools, and techniques to keep the studio at the cutting edge
  • Build and maintain an internal prompt library with documented best practices and style guidelines
  • Conduct A/B testing and iterative refinement to maximize output quality and consistency
  • Train team members on effective prompt engineering techniques and AI tool usage
Requirements
  • 1+ years of hands-on experience with generative AI tools (Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, DALL-E, Runway, etc.)
  • Strong understanding of prompt engineering principles, token optimization, and negative prompting
  • Excellent visual literacy ability to describe scenes, styles, lighting, and compositions precisely
  • Familiarity with ComfyUI, Automatic1111, or similar open-source AI workflows
  • Experience with at least one scripting language (Python preferred) for automation and batch processing
  • Strong communication skills and ability to translate creative direction into effective prompts
  • Portfolio demonstrating AI-generated work across different styles and use cases
Nice to Have
Experience with fine-tuning or training LoRA/DreamBooth models Knowledge of 3D rendering, VFX, or virtual production workflows Background in digital art, graphic design, or photography Familiarity with LLM APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic) for text-based automation
